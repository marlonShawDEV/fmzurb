Products

We offer the most flexible and innovative products with transparency and certainty of execution.

Freddie Mac provides a full range of competitively priced, reliable loan products for the acquisition, refinance or moderate rehabilitation of multifamily communities.

Conventional

Our Conventional offerings include fixed- and floating-rate loans, lease-up, value-add and moderate rehab loans, loans for manufactured housing communities and student housing, supplemental loans, structured loans and more.

Small Balance Loan

Our successful small balance loan program, for loans from $1 million to $6 million, offer streamlined processes during pricing, underwriting, closing and funding.

Targeted Affordable

We offer financing in underserved areas that are affordable to households with low and very-low incomes, including cash loans, bond credit enhancements, tax-exempt loans and others.

Seniors Housing

Seniors Housing financing presents complexities unique to this market. We understand them and offer loans for independent living properties, assisted living and memory care properties.

Lock Options

Freddie Mac Multifamily offers a variety of lock options including the Index Lock, Early Rate-Lock and Standard Delivery. Each choice provides various benefits and alternatives.

Green Advantage

Our new Green Advantage initiative rewards borrowers who improve their properties to save energy or water, or who already have green certified properties and are looking for financing.
