Conventional
Lenders

Freddie Mac purchases multifamily loans through the nation's best locally based lenders who have years of lending expertise and proven track records of success. Together, these lenders make up Freddie Mac's Approved Conventional Seller/Servicer network.

Small Balance Loan
Lenders

Freddie Mac Multifamily has a Seller/Servicer designation especially for Small Balance Loans (SBLs) in recognition of our offering that provides approved lenders a dedicated platform to originate and sell small multifamily loans of $1 million - $6 million and with at least five apartment units.

Targeted Affordable
Lenders

Companies approved by Freddie Mac to be Multifamily Approved Targeted Affordable Housing Seller/Servicers can sell and service Freddie Mac targeted affordable housing loans secured by properties located anywhere in the United States.

Seniors Housing
Lenders

Freddie Mac Multifamily has a Seller/Servicer designation especially for Seniors Housing in recognition of our program's ongoing importance and the necessary specialization required to support it effectively.
