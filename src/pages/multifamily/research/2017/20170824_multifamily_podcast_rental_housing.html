Steve and Aaron Explore Rental Housing Affordability

August 24, 2017

{{> share-widget}}

Steve Guggenmos (left) and Aaron Dunn

In the latest episode of the Freddie Mac Multifamily Podcast series, hosts Steve Guggenmos, vice president of Research and Modeling, and Aaron Dunn, head of Multifamily Investor Relations, define and discuss rental housing affordability. They also explain the use and importance of Low-income Housing Tax Credits (LIHTC).
