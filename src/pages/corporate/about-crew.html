Community Crew

The Freddie Mac Community Crew volunteers give freely of their time and talents in the neighborhoods where they live and work.

Freddie Mac Community Crew

GIVE. SERVE. IMPACT.

Anchored by month-long SERVE initiatives in April and October, the Community Crew takes part in a range of volunteer activities across the country that support our housing mission and our longstanding focus on improving financial literacy.

By working together with our nonprofit partners year-round, we help strengthen communities and deepen our company's roots in these communities. It's a bond that makes Freddie Mac a better neighbor and a better company.
